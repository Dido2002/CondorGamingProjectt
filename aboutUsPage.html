This Is Our Story

We’ve come a long way since we started our iGaming journey back in 2012. Today, with 9 brands and over a million users worldwide, we can proudly look back and say we’re ahead of the pack and our impact on this fast-evolving industry was significant.

2012
In the beginning...

Established in 2012, Condor Gaming Group swiftly became a leading brand within the world of Online Casino and Sportsbook operators. The company continues to ride high with multiple offices across Europe and over 100 staff members to date. This is our journey…

In 2012, Condor Gaming Group began its operations as a white label company with the launch of its first two brands Casino Sieger and Rembrandt Casino. Both brands became household names, which led to the 2014 inception of b-Bets, Condor Gaming Group’s sports-oriented brand and online casino.

2014

b-Bets, Condor Gaming Group’s first fully-fledged sports brand was launched in 2014. Incorporated along with Condor Gaming Group’s casino and live casino games was an immersive sportsbook that featured a vast array of sports to wager on from all over the world. Signature rewards in the form of BidBets were introduced along with an unparalleled Auction system where players could bet on specialized lot offers before the hammer drops.

2016
24Bettle

Fast forward to 2016, which saw the launch of another brand, 24Bettle. A considerable amount of attention was paid to the design and elements of the innovative gamification within this casino and sportsbook brand. The introduction of Carlos the Bull, 24Bettle’s own mascot, quickly became a customer favorite and led Condor to yet more success as they began to branch out across Europe.

2017
Condor Gaming Group Bulgaria

2017 was a big year for Condor Gaming Group. It opened its Tech Hub in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, was granted an MGA license, and saw the launch in the following year of its fifth and fiercest brand loaded with state-of-the-art gamification, Big5Casino.

UKGC License

As a fully self-sufficient operator, Condor Gaming Group has ensured that it maintains ownership of its entire platform to IP, together with its signature affiliate software. In 2017, Condor Gaming Group acquired its UKGC license through its decision to remain operating within the MGA Markets.

2019

Fast forward to 2019, Condor Gaming Group opened its Support and VIP hub in Rijeka, Croatia adding an additional 30 staff members to its ever-growing Condor Family.
